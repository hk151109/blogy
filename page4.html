Being unique is better than being perfect
By Sara Kings and Nicole Kings - April 27, 2012

I’m the first to admit it – I love unique, flawed, imperfect things. I crave the muffins that have overflowed unevenly, and have that yummy, crackly, crunchy bit on the muffin top. I prefer a field of wildflowers, weeds and all, over a manicured, perfect flower garden. And place awonky, homemade, baked-with-love cake in front of me at birthday time, and I’ll smile from ear to ear. So why don’t we cherish these imperfections in ourselves?
